Yongtao Hu

I'm currently a Staff Researcher at Image & Visual Computing Lab (IVCL) of Lenovo Research & Technology, Hong Kong. Before Join Lenovo, I obtained the Ph.D. degree of Computer Science from The University of Hong Kong in 2014. During the PhD study, I was working at the Computer Graphics Group under the supervision of Prof. Wenping Wang.

I was previously a research assistant (intern) at Image & Visual Computing Lab (IVCL) of Lenovo Research & Technology, Hong Kong during 2014-07 ~ 2014-11 and research intern at Internet Graphics Group of Microsoft Research Asia during 2010-03 ~ 2010-06. Before these, I graduated with a B.S. of Computer Science from Shandong University in 2010.

Research interests

My research is focused on multimedia and computer vision, with special interests in image/video analysis and processing, speaker detection and identification, multimodal data processing, TV/movie viewing experience enhancement, human and face detection, and motion tracking.
