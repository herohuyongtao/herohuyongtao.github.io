Yongtao Hu

I'm a Ph.D. candidate of Department of Computer Science in The University of Hong Kong. I'm currently working at the Computer Graphics Group under the supervision of Prof. Wenping Wang.

I was previously a research intern at Internet Graphics Group of Microsoft Research Asia during 2010-03 ~ 2010-06. I graduated with a B.S. in Computer Science from Shandong University in 2010.

Research interests

My research is focused on computer vision area, with special interests in image/video analysis and processing, speaker detection, audio processing, TV/movie viewing experience enhancement, human and face detection, and motion tracking.

Most of my time recently is spent on enhancing video (e.g. TV and movies) viewing experience enhancement by 1) creating dynamic subtitles that follow their respective speakers and 2) converting them into more interesting comics automatically.
